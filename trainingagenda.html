Training Agenda

  • Experience working with donors is one of the most important aspects of this job, which I gained working on a community archives project.
  • This position requires basic legal knowledge about donor relations: I want donors to be informed about their rights when they donate their artwork to us. For example, some say they don’t want their work to be available online.
  • It’s important to have good communication skills; conversations with donors can get personal very quickly. I’ll have conversations with donors about what does it mean to continue your legacy? What does it mean to have an afterlife? Do you have any lingering questions? Do you have a family member who will take care of your material after you pass away?
  • You have to learn how to navigate the relationship, because it feels close and you’re going in their home and the lines can get blurred between personal and professional
  • I’m out in the field often, traveling once a month, from 3 days to two weeks, to have in-person meetings with donors
